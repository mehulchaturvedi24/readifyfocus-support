Readify Focus – Support

Readify Focus is a simple reading-focus trainer designed to improve attention using short stories and interactive quizzes. No accounts, no ads, and no tracking — just focused learning.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account?
No. Everything works offline and stays on your device.

What does the paid unlock include?
It unlocks advanced focus levels (Level 4 & 5) and future premium levels.

Is my data stored online?
No. All progress and focus stats remain on your device only.

Will my purchase restore on a new iPhone?
Yes. Apple automatically restores your purchase.
