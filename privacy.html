Privacy Policy for Readify Focus

Last updated: 07 December 2025

Readify Focus (“the App”) is a simple, offline reading-attention trainer. The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Level progress
  • Focus statistics
  • Premium unlock state (managed by Apple)

No data is sent to any server or third party.

2. In-App Purchases

All payments are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. We do not access or store your Apple ID or payment information.

3. Children’s Privacy

The App contains no ads, no social features, and no tracking. It is suitable for general audiences.

4. Data Removal

Deleting the App removes all data permanently, as no information is stored externally.

5. Third-Party Services

Readify Focus does not use any third-party analytics, tracking frameworks, or external servers.
